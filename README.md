Digi – Scroll to Skill
Digi is a digital learning and mentorship platform designed to help students and learners gain knowledge directly from their mobile phones. The idea behind Digi is simple: turn scrolling time into learning time — that’s why we call it “Scroll to Skill.”
The platform focuses on learning, mentoring, advising, and guiding students so they can improve their academic performance and follow the right career path.
Mission
Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, mentorship, and guidance through accessible digital platforms.
We believe learning should be:
Accessible
Practical
Mobile-friendly
Community-driven
Current Focus
The project starts with a scheduled outreach event aimed at supporting freshman students.
Before launching full mentorship and learning programs, Digi focuses on:
Helping students improve their academic performance
Guiding them to score better grades
Providing study roadmaps
Helping them choose the right academic field
Supporting students with practical advice and guidance
This is the first phase of the Digi project, which will later expand into a full learning and mentorship ecosystem.
